What is a Trading Pool?

Instead of trading alone, we combine our capital into one big pool. This gives us more power to enter higher-tier trades and share the profits proportionally.

  • Step 1: You send your entry fee to the official M-Pesa or Binance address above.
  • Step 2: You get a unique Entry Code (e.g., POOL-045) – this tracks your spot.
  • Step 3: Once 100 people have joined, the pool officially kicks off on 1st July 2026.
  • Step 4: calekyzFX trades the combined capital. Every trade is tracked and transparent.
  • Step 5: Profits (after fees) are shared proportionally back to every participant based on their entry size.

✅ All entries are confirmed via WhatsApp for your security and record.
